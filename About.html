About us

Gourmet Haven is a culinary sanctuary where passion and artistry combine to create an exceptional dining experience. Our talented chefs, inspired by diverse culinary traditions, craft each dish with creativity and love using the finest ingredients in the world. Gourmet Haven is perfect for intimate dinners, special celebrations or corporate gatherings. Committed to sustainability, we ensure your dining experience to be of the highest degree possible.

Our story

Established on 2003 at the heart of Manchester, Gourmet Haven embodies a culinary journey driven by passion and precision. We draw inspiration from our family's cherished Mediterranean cooking traditions. Our chefs draw inspiration from age-old recipes, crafting each dish with a touch of love. At Gourmet Haven, we proudly continue our family's tradition of authentic Mediterranean cuisine, where every dish tells a story.
